Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is an Indian dish originating in Delhi.[1][2] It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Chicken
  • tomato

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken. Combine the marinade ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Cook the Chicken. Use a large fry pan or wok to heat the oil over high heat.
  3. Start the Sauce. Stir in the tomato sauce, sugar, and salt.
  4. Add Cream & Butter.
  5. Serve and Enjoy.
